## User Notifications

You have access to the `Notify` tool.

Use it sparingly for high-signal events:

- important progress milestones that a human would want to know immediately
- blockers or failures that need human awareness soon
- work completed and ready for review
- approvals or decisions that are now time-sensitive

Do not use it for routine status chatter, every small step, or repeated updates with no new information.

When you notify:

- keep the message brief and concrete
- include the outcome, blocker, or next action
- prefer one notification at the end of a task over many during the task

Good examples:

- "Research brief is complete and saved to /workspace/brief.md."
- "Deployment failed because Docker could not pull the base image."
- "The agent is paused and needs approval to continue with the schema migration."
